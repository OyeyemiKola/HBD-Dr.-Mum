You Truly Care

I love children, honestly i do, they are one of the most rewarding ways to spend your time, knowing you're impacting them positively for life.

But, I still can't compare to how much she loves children, not just love but genuinely cares, not just the one she knows like her children, or her friend's children or her nephews, that's what makes it more special.

I've seen it personally, moments where she genuinely cares, from slowing down or completely stopping with cars behind her honking for students to cross the roads when others sped past like nothing happened, or interferring when two kids where being harassed by an adult, or correcting students who were carelessly playing or fighting on the road or the most common one, when she reaches into her pocket to give to those who ask or those who don't.

It's makes you think differently, every correction I don't want to hear, every advice she gives when I'm not ready to listen, every criticism, every nag, anger. Itis all because she genuinely cares, and it's said we don't see it that way, it's even worse when we don't appreciate it.


- Kola Oyeyemi
