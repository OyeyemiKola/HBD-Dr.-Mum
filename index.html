About Her

Dr. Abe grew up in a warzone - seven older siblings and two parents? That's a recipe for chaos. But she wasn't fazed. Even as a kid, she had this crazy superpower - she could see right through people's cracks. Not the bad kind, though. Like a mechanic spotting a loose engine bolt.

Fast forward 30 years. She's a licensed doctor, rocking two kids of her own and still taking care of everyone else's. She's got this fierce love, like a mama bear with a heart of gold. Flaws? Don't even get her started. They're like flashing neon signs screaming "Fix me!" to her. And fix you, she will. Maybe you want her help, maybe you don't. Either way, she's got your back, even if it means dragging you kicking and screaming to a better life.

Need medical help? She's got you covered. Need someone to knock some sense into you about that bad decision you made last night? No problem. She has seen it all,done it all and learnt from it. She's the doctor who heals bodies, but more importantly, she's the doctor who prepares you for life's toughest battles.
